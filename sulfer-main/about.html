About Us

iMentorU Foundation NPO/NPC was founded by Mabale Tinyiko Jr. Miracle (Executive Director & Chairperson) in 2017, with the purpose and passion of helping many young people in developing areas, but as the organisation grew, we saw the need to accommodate every young individual - globally, who are in need, who are clueless about the strategies of life, careers, education, talents and skills, to develop the greatest mindset, to positively influence, change, empower, guide, support, teach, motivate and inspire more youth in a form of youth development and mentorship.

With more members joining in as mentors and more related organisations getting involved as partners, iMentorU Foundation has become a national recognised organisation, adopting schools, communities and undividuals in South Africa.

Why we exist

iMentorU Foundation is a Youth Development & Mentorship organisation existing for better Inspiration, Leadership, Success, Change, Fun, Problem Solving, Independency and Support to the youth. We aim to be the leading positive influential organisation globally, that impact mostly on the unprivileged youths, we are not to compete with any related organisations but to connect, promote and build strong relationships with all related organisations to better empower and inspire the young groups.

Our Mission

iMentorU Foundation is committed to, by any means - be there for every young individuals, to give as much support as we can, and partnering with more related organisations globally, to help youth build their self-esteem, self-confidence, self-disciplines and determinations towards their own goals, talents, passions and dreams - personally and professionally.

Our Vision

As an inspiring, positive and motivated organisation, we foresee the great change in the communities and young people, our greatest vision is to Enrich, Inspire, Empower as many young people as we can, in education, careers, creativity / talents, fun and life in general by giving them as much support as we can through our development and mentorship programmes

We are committed to *being there* for every young
individual amongst those we reach out to, to give as much support as we can and to partner with related organizations globally.
