About Us

iMentorU Foundation is a Youth Development & Mentorship organisation existing for better Inspiration, Leadership, Success, Change, Fun, Problem Solving, Independency and Support to the youth. We aim to be the leading positive influential organisation globally, that impact mostly on the unprivileged youths, we are not to compete with any related organisations but to connect, promote and build strong relationships with all related organisations to better empower and inspire the young groups.

In the world with so many challenges, we are committed to remain positive and keep inspiring more young people and communities globally. Our target groups ranges between the ages of 8 – 35 years, which includes but not limited to individuals who are:Creative, Innovative, Disabled, Underprivileged and Talented

Our Core Values
